## Who is this site for?

The Claude Codex covers a lot of ground: installation, prompting, MCPs, agents, enterprise, security... With so much content, where do you start?

These learning paths answer that question. Each one targets a specific profile and offers a logical reading route through the site's pages. You don't need to read everything: focus on the steps that match your daily work.

<Callout type="tip" title="No need to pick just one path">
You're a developer AND a freelancer? A tech lead AND curious about non-dev prompting? Mix and match paths freely. They're here to guide, not to box you in.
</Callout>

## The five profiles

**The developer** wants to code faster. Their path covers installation, effective prompting, development MCPs, and agents.

**The tech lead** wants to deploy Claude Code across their team. They need governance, security, progressive adoption, and CI/CD integration.

**The non-developer** is opening the terminal for the first time. Their path starts with the basics and moves at their own pace toward concrete use cases.

**The freelancer** wants to make every hour count. Plugins, automation, the configurator, and MCP workflows are at the center of their path.

**The student** learns by building. Their journey combines fundamentals, hands-on projects, and professional best practices.

## How to use these paths

Each path page follows the same structure:

1. **Who you are**: the target profile in a few sentences
2. **Your journey**: steps in the recommended order, with direct links
3. **Next steps**: where to go once the path is complete

Steps are numbered but flexible. If you already know a topic, skip straight to the next one.
